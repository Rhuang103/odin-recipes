Steak

An inch thick steak, grilled with salt and black pepper

Ingredients

  • 12 oz Ribeye Steak 1-inch thick
  • 1 Tsp salt
  • 1 Tsp Black Pepper
  • 1 TBS Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Heat a 10 inch castiron pan until it smokes
  2. Add in the oil and swirl pan to coat the surface with oil
  3. Add salt and black pepper to both sides of the steak
  4. Place steak in the pan
  5. Flip every 1 minute until at desired doneness or to 120 F
  6. Take out the steak and let it rest for 5 mins
