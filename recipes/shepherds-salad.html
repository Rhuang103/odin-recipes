Shepherd's Salad

A Turkish Salad with Cucumber, Tomatoes, Onions, and Cilantro

Ingredients

  • 1 long english cucumber
  • 4 Campari Tomatoes
  • 1/4 of a red onion
  • 1 sprig of cilantro
  • 1/4 of a lemon
  • olive oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Dice the cucumber and tomato
  2. Mince the onion and cilantro
  3. Add cucumber, tomato, onion and cilantro into a large bowl
  4. Squeeze the juice of the lemon over the vegetables
  5. Add salt and olive oil to taste
