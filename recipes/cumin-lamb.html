Cumin Lamb

Lamb leg thin sliced, stir-fryed with cumin, sichuan peppercorn, ginger, and garlic.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 pound of lamb leg
  • Half a whole yellow onion
  • 4 Cloves of garlic
  • 0.5 inch of ginger
  • 1 spring of cilantro
  • 2 TBS of half ground cumin
  • 1 TBS of sichuan peppercorn
  • 1 TBS of korean red pepper powder
  • 0.5 Tsp of sugar
  • 1 Tsp of salt
  • 1 TBS of rice wine
  • 1 TBS of Soy Sauce
  • 1 TBS of cooking oil

Steps

  1. Slice the lamb leg into thin slices
  2. Marinate in a bowl with rice wine and soy sauce for 30 mins
  3. Mince the garlic and ginger
  4. Cut the onion into slices
  5. Cut the cilantro into half inch sections
  6. Mix in a small bowl the cumin, sichuan peppercorn, red pepper powder, and sugar
  7. Heat up and skillet and add oil
  8. Add in the ginger and garlic and stir fry until fragrant
  9. Add in the lamb and stir fry until cooked
  10. Add the salt and spice mixture
  11. Mix together well and serve
